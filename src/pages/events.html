Centralizing Refugee Crisis Efforts in Sussex

Monday February 22nd 2016, 7–9:30pm | 68 Middle street, Brighton

As a takeaway from last month's meetup, we decided to focus February's event on centralizing refugee crisis efforts across Sussex (free food and drink will be provided by our sponsors).

We are hoping to hear from a variety of refugee community organizations with the ultimate aim of creating a broader community of support to help refugees prosper in Brighton and Sussex.
