Our Journey

Prosper emerged out of one designer's response to the human suffering of the refugee crisis in Europe. Since launching in October 2015, a global community of skilled digital workers has centralized around a single mission:

To transform the refugee crisis into an opportunity to foster a new Europe—a society where every person is cherished and prosperity abounds.

Open-Source Projects

Prosper Projects are a focal point of our community. They are a combination of human-centered design and open source technology and are built and maintained by online volunteers from all over the world. Each Prosper Project is designed to fulfill a few criteria:

  • Solve a real human problem of the refugee crisis
  • Be sustainable, scalable and significant
  • Solutions must be measurable

Volunteer

As a skilled digital worker, there are problems of the refugee crisis that only you can solve...

The power of design and technology has placed digital workers in a unique position for humanitarian crises—the refugee crisis in particular. If you have skills, passion and experience you can make a massive impact and have fun alongside a diverse community of passionate professionals.

We've worked hard to make sure that our volunteers can easily contribute to refugee crisis projects no matter where your are in the world. As a result, we've build a vibrant community of skilled digital workers all over the world who are working together to help refugees prosper in Europe.

Introducing Prosper Academy:

A program for refugees who want to land full-time jobs as digital workers in Europe.

In a sector that has more jobs than people to fill them, we've designed a solution for refugees to participate in world-class job training while transitioning to a new life in Europe. Rather than settle for stacking shelves, our apprentices are learning the craft that drives leading businesses around the world: Creating user-centered applications on the web.
