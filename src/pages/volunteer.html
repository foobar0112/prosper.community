Volunteer

As a skilled digital worker, there are problems of the refugee crisis that only you can solve...

The power of design and technology has placed digital workers in a unique position for humanitarian crises—the refugee crisis in particular. If you have skills, passion and experience you can make a massive impact and have fun alongside a diverse community of passionate professionals.

We've worked hard to make sure that our volunteers can easily contribute to meaningful projects of the refugee crisis no matter where they are in the world. As a result, we've build a vibrant global community of skilled digital workers who are collaborating to help refugees prosper in Europe.

Whether you're able to offer an hour a week or ten—we make it easy for skilled digital workers to help refugees prosper. As a volunteer you can:

  • Contribute to one of our projects and/or
  • Mentor an apprentice

We have immediate need in the following disciplines:

  • Ethnography & User Research
  • Agile Project Management
  • Human-Centered Design
  • Web Development & Architecture
  • Social Media & Digital Marketing
  • Others*

*If your skillset isn't listed above and you'd like to get involved, please contact us. The need for passionate professionals with relevant skills is immense. We enjoy matching skilled workers with meaningful projects that make a significant impact on helping the refugee crisis.
