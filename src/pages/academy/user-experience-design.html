Overview

The User Experience Design track at Prosper Academy is an apprenticeship for budding designers who want to grow their UX skills and maturity through contributing to refugee crisis projects.

custom curriculum

Rather than study a set curriculum like other design courses, our apprentices work with a career coach to develop a custom skill plan based upon their design experience, career goals and lifestyle needs.

With a custom skill plan in place, the apprentice gains experience and maturity through collaborating with other designers and developers on a Prosper Project. Every week, the apprentice gets to meet with their mentor and the project team. The program structure allows apprentices to learn how to work in an Agile environment and how to design web-based products with Lean methodologies.

this track is designed for:

  • Designers who are passionate about learning how to use human-centered design to solve real problems for real people.

  • Citizens and refugees who have experience creating websites / applications and want to grow UX skills and maturity

  • Self-guided learners who know the value of collaborating with a mentor and other designers

  • Designers who can perservere through thorny problems to craft elegant solutions

this track is not designed for:

  • Complete beginners with no experience designing websites.

  • People who need hand-holding to complete tasks on real projects.

  • People who discriminate against asylum seekers and migrants.

Skill Plans

While we do create custom skill plans for each apprentice, we have found it helpful to provide some sample skill plans for apprentices to pick and choose the types of skills they'd like to learn. Feel free to use these plans as a reference for the type of plan you'll customize with your career coach when you start the program.

strategic ux

I want to craft the strategy of a digital product's design and development—working with other designers to articulate and execute a vision defined by business requirements and user needs.

  • Stakeholder research
  • Workshop design and facilitation
  • Impact mapping
  • Lean canvas
  • Value proposition design
  • Storytelling for UX
  • Articulating design decisions

tactical ux

I want to focus on the craft of interaction design. My background in art has given me a strong foundation in visual design and I want to learn how to apply it to designing the interface of digital products.

  • Curating artistic direction
  • Interface sketching
  • Prototyping
  • User testing
  • Pitching design concepts
  • Providing design critique

ux research

I want to understand what motivates people to do what they do, and I want to be able to articulate real human needs to project stakeholders and to the product team. I want to play an instrumental role in creating digital products that people love.

  • Ethnography
  • User interviews
  • Stakeholder interviews
  • User testing
  • Competitor research and analysis
  • Diary studies
  • Survey design and analysis
